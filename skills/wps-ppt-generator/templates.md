# PPT Style Templates

A catalog of style presets that can be referenced during Stage 3 (Design Spec). These are style descriptions, not file-based templates.

## Available Styles

### 1. Tech Product Launch
- **Tag**: tech-launch
- **Description**: High-contrast minimalist theme. Large sans-serif titles, dark background, bold white text, with high-clarity product visuals. Creates strong visual impact similar to Apple/Xiaomi keynotes.
- **Colors**: Black background, white text, orange accent
- **Typography**: Bold sans-serif headers, clean body text

### 2. Corporate Annual Report
- **Tag**: corporate-report
- **Description**: Professional data-driven layout with structured grids. Navy/dark blue tones with gold accents. Emphasis on charts, tables, and KPI metrics.
- **Colors**: Navy background, white/light gray text, gold accent
- **Typography**: Serif headers, sans-serif body

### 3. Startup Pitch Deck
- **Tag**: startup-pitch
- **Description**: Bold gradient backgrounds with vibrant accent colors. Dynamic layouts with large numbers and minimal text. Designed to capture investor attention.
- **Colors**: Deep purple/blue gradient, white text, bright green/coral accent
- **Typography**: Heavy weight sans-serif throughout

### 4. Academic / Research
- **Tag**: academic
- **Description**: Clean white background with structured content blocks. Emphasis on readability with proper citation formatting. Charts and diagrams for data presentation.
- **Colors**: White background, dark gray text, blue accent
- **Typography**: Serif headers, sans-serif body, monospace for data

### 5. Education / Training
- **Tag**: education
- **Description**: Warm, approachable design with clear visual hierarchy. Uses icons and simple illustrations to aid understanding. Step-by-step layouts for processes.
- **Colors**: Light warm background, dark text, teal/green accent
- **Typography**: Rounded sans-serif headers, readable body text

### 6. Marketing Campaign
- **Tag**: marketing
- **Description**: Eye-catching design with bold typography and vibrant colors. Asymmetric layouts with strong visual focal points. Designed for brand storytelling.
- **Colors**: Brand-aligned palette with high saturation accents
- **Typography**: Display/decorative headers, clean body text

### 7. Government / Policy Report
- **Tag**: government
- **Description**: Formal and authoritative with structured layouts. Red/navy tones with clear data presentation. Strict hierarchy and consistent formatting.
- **Colors**: White/light gray background, navy text, red accent
- **Typography**: Standard serif/sans-serif, conservative sizing

### 8. Creative / Design Portfolio
- **Tag**: creative
- **Description**: Full-bleed visual layouts with minimal text overlay. Strong use of whitespace and typography as design elements. Grid-based image showcasing.
- **Colors**: Neutral base with one bold accent color
- **Typography**: Thin/light weight display fonts, minimal body text

## Usage

During Stage 3, Claude should consider the user's topic, audience, and goal to select or blend from these style presets. The style description informs the `<design_style>` field in the design spec. These are starting points - Claude should adapt and customize based on specific user needs rather than copying them verbatim.
